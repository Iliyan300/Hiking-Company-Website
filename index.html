Choose your trail:

Whether you are a beginner or a seasoned mountaineer, we have the perfect adventure for you.

Vitosha


The transition from Sofia to Vitosha is easy and one of the most popular mountain routes in Bulgaria. You can reach the capital's Black Peak and enjoy beautiful views. The panoramic views of Sofia and the surrounding areas from higher elevations are breathtaking.

Beginner

Rila


The trek through Rila Mountain provides a more demanding experience. One of the popular routes includes the transition from the "Seven Rila Lakes" hut to the "Rila Monastery" hut. The mountain is known for its breathtaking natural landscapes.

Intermediate

Pirin


For more experienced mountaineers, the trek through Pirin is a challenge. One of the most difficult routes includes the transition from the "Vihren" hut to the "Bunderitsa" hut. Trekking in the Pirin Mountains in Bulgaria is a unforgettable experience.

Expert

Quick Reminder

Remember that before going on a mountain trek, you should always prepare, check the weather and get the necessary equipment and supplies. Also, be careful and follow all safe mountain practices.
